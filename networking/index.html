Problem

Regional networking environments needed consistent operational support, accurate infrastructure records and coordinated delivery across local offices, vendors, ISPs and global systems teams.

The Sydney headquarters also required a major networking refresh covering firewall and wireless infrastructure while maintaining supportability for the office environment.

Project Examples

APJ Network Operations

Owned operational support for Cisco Meraki infrastructure, ISP management, vendor coordination and incident resolution across APJ environments.

Sydney HQ Refresh

Led onsite delivery for the replacement of dual Palo Alto PA-850 firewalls and Cisco Meraki wireless infrastructure within the Sydney headquarters office.

Approach

Onsite Delivery

Readiness & Handover

Implementation

Maintain regional visibility

Coordinate refresh delivery

Focused on supportability, observability, lifecycle management and handover needs after implementation activity was complete.

Outcome

The work improved supportability, observability, lifecycle management and wireless performance across the Sydney office environment while strengthening operational governance across APJ networking sites.

Lessons Learned

Strong network operations depend on both technical execution and accurate operational context: ownership, records, vendor paths and handover details need the same attention as the hardware refresh.
