Professional Work

APJ Operations

Regional technology operations ownership supporting approximately 190 employees across four APJ locations, while partnering with global IT teams serving more than 3,000 employees.

Back to projects

Problem

APJ operations spanned Sydney, Melbourne, Singapore and Tokyo, with different offices, time zones, vendors and local requirements. For approximately 18 months I operated as the sole APJ IT engineer and primary regional escalation point across IT services.

Maintaining continuity required more than resolving individual requests. Regional support, onboarding, asset management, procurement, workplace technology and vendor operations needed repeatable systems that aligned local execution with global standards.

Scope & Role

Regional Ownership

Owned end-to-end regional operations and served as the primary escalation point for APJ technology services.

Multi-country Support

Supported approximately 190 employees across Australia, Singapore and Japan within a global SaaS environment.

Operational Breadth

Covered identity, SaaS, endpoints, onboarding, assets, procurement, networking and workplace technology.

Global Partnership

Worked with Systems, Security, Workplace, Finance, Procurement, Legal and People teams supporting 3,000+ employees.

Operational Systems

Onboarding and offboarding framework

Designed a structured framework covering preparation, provisioning, employee guidance and supporting documentation. The approach was later adopted globally and adapted for device replacements and contractor onboarding.

Regional inventory management

Built an inventory system spanning the APJ office footprint, introducing stock tracking, forecasting, automated replenishment, financial visibility and operational reporting.

IT asset disposition

Established governance, vendor management, inventory reconciliation, chain-of-custody controls and quarterly routines for compliant device retirement.

IT support quality

Analysed approximately 300 support tickets to identify process, troubleshooting and service maturity gaps, then developed support journey maps, coaching approaches and improvement frameworks.

Leadership & Delivery

Technical Leadership

Onboarded and mentored APJ, EMEA and AMER colleagues and coached new Help Desk team members.

Knowledge & Enablement

Delivered recurring sessions on internal systems, operational architecture, AI tooling and regional practices.

Vendor & Commercial Management

Led vendor evaluation, RFPs, replacement, onboarding and governance across Australia, Singapore and Japan.

Cross-functional Execution

Aligned local requirements with security, finance, procurement, legal, people and global technology stakeholders.

Outcomes

Operational Continuity

Maintained coverage across four APJ locations during an extended period as the sole regional engineer.

32+ Onboarding Cycles

Delivered repeatable onboarding while improving the framework used for future employee lifecycle work.

AUD $10K Asset Recovery

Returned approximately AUD $10,000 through the structured IT asset disposition programme.

20+ Significant Projects

Delivered work across operations, infrastructure, workplace technology, automation and governance.

The capability demonstrated here is not simply high-volume support. It is the ability to maintain regional continuity while converting recurring work into documented, measurable and transferable operating systems.
