Overview

Workplace technology sits across infrastructure, user experience and operations. A successful environment requires networks, room systems, workplace resources, devices and support processes to work as one service rather than as isolated technology components.

My work has included new office commissioning, office relocation and expansion, meeting-room deployment, high-impact AV redesign, vendor management, procurement, documentation and operational handover across APJ.

This is supported by broader regional facilities experience coordinating incidents, maintenance, compliance, budgets, service providers and technical infrastructure within international workplace environments and office redevelopment projects.
