Professional Work

Workplace Technology

Led the redesign and replacement of a high-profile Sydney town hall environment, transforming a manual AV setup into a fully automated Crestron-powered collaboration platform.

Back to projects

Problem

A high-profile executive and company-wide presentation environment depended on a highly manual, IT-dependent AV setup. The experience needed to become more reliable, easier to operate and scalable enough for repeatable company-wide communication.

Approach

Environment Redesign

Led the complete redesign and replacement of the executive and company-wide presentation environment.

Automation

Replaced manual AV operation with a modern, automated Crestron-powered collaboration platform.

Self-service Operation

Designed for a self-service experience that reduced IT dependency during recurring presentation workflows.

Sustainability

Produced technical, operational and end-user documentation to support long-term maintainability.

Implementation

Assess the existing operating model

Identified where manual setup, IT dependency and inconsistent operating steps created risk for high-visibility events.

Deliver the replacement platform

Coordinated the redesign and replacement activity around an automated Crestron collaboration platform.

Prepare for ongoing use

Documented technical configuration, operating procedures and end-user workflows so the environment could be supported beyond the project.

Outcome

The environment moved from a manual, support-heavy setup to a more reliable self-service collaboration experience, improving usability, supportability and scalability for executive and company-wide presentations.

Lessons Learned

Workplace technology projects succeed when the room, the platform and the operating model are designed together. Automation only creates durable value when the support path and user experience are clear.
