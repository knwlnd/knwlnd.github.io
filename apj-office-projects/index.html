Professional Work

APJ Office Projects

Office technology delivery across APJ, including Singapore office commissioning and Tokyo office relocation and expansion work.

Back to projects

Problem

Office projects compress many dependencies into a fixed delivery window: procurement, local vendors, workplace readiness, stakeholder expectations, support handoff and business continuity all have to line up before the space can operate well.

Project Examples

Singapore Office IT Commission

Commissioning work covering readiness planning, procurement coordination, vendor management and the practical handoff from build activity into day-to-day operations.

Tokyo Office IT Relocation & Expansion

Relocation and expansion support balancing local delivery constraints, stakeholder alignment, vendor coordination and operational continuity.

Approach

Ambiguity

Moved work forward while requirements, timelines and dependencies continued to change.

Stakeholder Management

Aligned office, regional, vendor and internal technology needs into practical delivery decisions.

Procurement

Coordinated technology purchasing, lead times, availability and readiness constraints.

Vendor Coordination

Managed external handoffs so implementation work matched operational expectations.

Implementation

Define readiness requirements

Translated office needs into practical technology, support and handoff requirements.

Coordinate local delivery

Worked across vendors, internal teams and office stakeholders to keep delivery moving.

Prepare for operations

Focused on what needed to be supportable once the project activity was complete.

Outcome

The work helped turn office project activity into operationally usable spaces, with clearer procurement, delivery and support handoffs across regional office projects.

Lessons Learned

Successful office technology delivery depends on surfacing dependencies early, keeping stakeholders aligned and treating operational handover as part of the project rather than an afterthought.
