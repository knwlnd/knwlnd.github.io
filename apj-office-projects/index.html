Professional Work

APJ Office Projects

End-to-end ownership of APJ office technology projects, including Singapore office commissioning, Tokyo office relocation and expansion, and a complete redesign of the Sydney Town Hall AV environment.

Back to projects

Problem

Business growth across APJ created a range of workplace technology needs: establishing a new Southeast Asia hub in Singapore, relocating and expanding the Tokyo office, and replacing an unreliable, manually operated AV setup at the primary APJ headquarters in Sydney.

Each project required technology planning, budgeting, vendor selection, procurement, implementation and documentation to be coordinated against a fixed delivery window without compromising the usability or ongoing supportability of the finished environment.

Project Examples

Singapore Office Commissioning

Established the complete office technology environment for a new 6-to-30-person Southeast Asia hub, with an indirect impact across the wider APJ region.

Tokyo Office Relocation & Expansion

Relocated and expanded the technology environment for a growing 10-to-20-person Tokyo office while maintaining readiness for regional operations.

Sydney Town Hall AV Redesign

Redesigned and replaced an unreliable, fully manual video-conferencing environment at the primary APJ headquarters, supporting approximately 130 employees.

Singapore Office

Problem & Scope

A new Singapore office was required as the Southeast Asia hub to support business growth. The office was designed to scale from approximately 6 to 30 people, with an indirect operational impact across APJ.

My Role

Held complete ownership of the IT workstream from end to end, including planning, budgeting, vendor RFP, procurement, implementation, commissioning and documentation.

Technology Delivery

Coordinated network configuration with the vendor and delivered Neat meeting-room hardware, LG displays, workstations and CCTV. Created and configured Zoom Rooms and Google Workspace resources for the new spaces.

Outcome

The office technology project was delivered on time, to specification and within budget.

Tokyo Office

Problem & Scope

The existing Tokyo office needed to be relocated and expanded to support growth. The new environment supported approximately 10 to 20 people and had an indirect impact across APJ.

My Role

Held complete ownership of the IT workstream from initial planning through budgeting, vendor RFP, procurement, implementation, commissioning and final documentation.

Technology Delivery

Coordinated network configuration with the vendor and delivered Neat meeting-room hardware, LG displays, workstations and CCTV. Created and configured Zoom Rooms and Google Workspace resources for the relocated office.

Outcome

The relocation and expansion were delivered on time, to specification and within budget.

Sydney Town Hall AV Redesign

Problem & Scope

The existing video-conferencing setup was difficult to use, fully manual and unreliable, requiring IT involvement for every event. The Town Hall is a high-impact space at the primary APJ headquarters, supporting an office of approximately 130 employees.

My Role

Owned the project end to end in consultation with the Global Director of IT. This included the full redesign and replacement, planning, budgeting, vendor RFP, procurement, implementation, commissioning, documentation and driving user adoption.

Technology Delivery

Coordinated network VLAN assignment, whitelisting and SecureW2 allowlisting. Delivered and commissioned Crestron, QSC, Logitech and Shure hardware, then created and configured the Zoom Room and associated Google Workspace resource.

Outcome

Replaced the manual environment with an integrated, user-friendly AV system. The project was delivered on time, to specification and within budget.

Delivery Approach

End-to-End Ownership

Managed each IT workstream from requirements and budget through implementation and documentation.

Vendor & Procurement Management

Ran vendor RFPs, evaluated delivery options and coordinated procurement, lead times and installation.

Commissioning & Validation

Validated physical installation, platform configuration and room readiness before operational handover.

Operational Readiness

Completed documentation, support handoff and adoption work so each environment could be used effectively.

Overall Outcome

All three projects were delivered on time, to specification and within budget. The work created scalable technology environments in Singapore and Tokyo and transformed the Sydney Town Hall into a more reliable, user-friendly space that no longer depended on IT for routine operation.
